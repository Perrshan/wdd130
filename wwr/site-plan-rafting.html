Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help those seeking for adventure to have an easy place to find great locations where they can go white water rafting with our company! We hope to connect with those who are looking for a thrilling and safe ride with our experienced team. We want everyone to know about our amazing company!

Audience

The Audience would be those who are looking for a thrilling and safe ride down one of our guided rafting trips. 1. Who are the target customers? Anyone looking for the chance to go WWR and have a thrill of a time. Normally ages 12-50, church youngmen groups included. 2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find nearby locations including drop off and pick up where they can go on a guided WWR trip. They will also find prices of that trip. How many people can go on the trip. Available dates and times. What they need to do to prepare for the trip. Amount of time the trip will take. Information about the guides. 3. How will my audience usually access my site? I would expect anything with a screen would be used to access the website so using em or % measurements will be important. Be weary of the differing sizes.
