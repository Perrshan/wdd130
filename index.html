Idaho Falls, Idaho

Idaho Falls is the largest city in the Eastern Idaho region and is the third-largest metropolitan area in the state behind the Boise City-Nampa and Coeur d'Alene metropolitan areas. In the past decade, it has been surpassed in population by Meridian and Nampa, making Idaho Falls the fifth -largest city in Idaho.
